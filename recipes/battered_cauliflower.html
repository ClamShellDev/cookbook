Batter-Dipped Fried Cauliflower

A fried cauliflower meal fit as an appitizer or main course.

Ingredients

  • cauliflower – 1 head
  • cayenne – to taste
  • coarse-grain flour – 1 & 1/2 cups
  • dipping sauce – ranch/bleu cheese
  • eggs – 2 beaten
  • garlic poweder – 1/4 teaspoon
  • grated parmesan – 2 tablespoons
  • high smoke-point oil – 2 to 3 inches
  • hor d'oeuvres – matchstick carrots/celery
  • milk – 1 tablespoon
  • oregano – 1 teaspoon
  • pepper – 1/4 teaspoon
  • salt – 1/4 teaspoon
  • smoked paprika – 1/8 teaspoon
  • thyme – 1/2 teaspoon
  • tossing sauce – dealers choice

Steps

Step 1
Chop Cauliflower & Heat Oil (prep, 5 minutes)

Ingredients: cauliflower (1 head), high smoke-point oil (2 to 3 inches)

Rinse cauliflower then remove florets and discard stem. Preheat fryer oil to 375 (F).

Step 2
Prepare Wet Batter (prep, 3 minutes)

Ingredients: eggs (2 beaten), milk (1 tablespoon), salt (1/4 teaspoon)

In a bowl or bag add eggs, milk and salt. Stir/beat to combine.

Prepare Dry Batter (prep, 3 minutes)

Ingredients: coarse-grain flour (1 & 1/2 cups), grated parmesan (2 tablespoons), garlic poweder (1/4 teaspoon), thyme (1/2 teaspoon), oregano (1 teaspoon), smoked paprika (1/8 teaspoon), cayenne (to taste), pepper (1/4 teaspoon)

In another bowl or bag mix together flour, cheese and spices.

Step 3
Coat Cauliflower (prep, 10 minutes)

Coat florets in wet then dry batter ingredients making sure to cover fully

Fry Cauliflower (cook, 20 minutes)

Placed battered florets carefully in preheated oil in batches for 4-6 minutes or until golden brown. Drain/dry on wire rack or paper towel lined tray.

Step 4
Finish Cauliflower (combine, 2 minutes)

Ingredients: tossing sauce (dealers choice), dipping sauce (ranch/bleu cheese), hor d'oeuvres (matchstick carrots/celery)

Toss florets in sauce of choice and serve with ranch/bleu cheese and carrots/celery.
